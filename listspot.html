Booking Fee Model

Instead of taking a percentage from your earnings, SharaSpot charges a small booking fee per session (₹10–₹20 depending on location type).

  • You keep 100% of the charging session fee.
  • The booking fee is paid by the customer on top of the session amount.
  • Example: For a ₹150 session, the host receives the full ₹150, and SharaSpot collects a ₹15 booking fee separately.
